Folk Musician

Music keeps me sane. While I take on writing and web design projects for class, I play the fiddle as a way to take my mind off the other stress in my life. I started playing with my dad around 4 years old, and haven't stopped since. I play session tunes and dance tunes from a variety of traditions including old time, New England, Irish, Scottish, and Québécois. Find me at festivals, jams, dances, and concerts, on stage and in the back room. Look below for a calendar of past music events and samples of my music. At the moment, I'm between bands, so contact me if you're interested in playing together, or hearing my music at an event!
