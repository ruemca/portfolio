Web Content Developer

My interest in web technology stems from classes in high school, when I discovered I could publish my writing on websites only I could see. I'm less shy sharing my work now, but I still love experimenting with new elements of design and function, and seeing how they can interact with content.

My Content Development certificate is not an afterthought to my English degree, but an integral part of it. I always prefer to design with a content-first approach, and take particular interest in universal design strategies to include multiple devices and all abilities. Check out samples of my academic and professional work below, as well as my web resume.
