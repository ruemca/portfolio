ABOUT ME

Thanks for checking out my website!

I’m a student at Western Washington University in Bellingham, pursuing a degree in Creative Writing with a minor in Linguistics, and a certificate in Web Content Development. This website, as well as my degree, emphasizes the multidisciplinary diversity that I value in life.

There is so much in the world that needs attention, and I can only do so much on my own. I see myself first and foremost as an engaged citizen, an empathetic human being, who can listen to the people struggling most, and listen to the people working hardest, and support them as best I can. This could mean working and writing directly for a human rights organization, or it could mean creating art that reflects and amplifies the voices of those fighting at the front lines of social and environmental justice. At this early stage in my life, I don’t pretend to know exactly what my future holds. Whatever I do, however, I know I will be proud of it.
